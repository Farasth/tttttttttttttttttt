Our Clients

Smart Skool, developed by Klopterz Technologies Pvt. Ltd., is an advanced school management application designed to strengthen governance and communication across educational institutions. It offers a robust suite of features that enable school administrators, parents, and other stakeholders to effectively monitor student progress, track academic and extracurricular activities, and manage day-to-day operations with ease. By centralizing key functions and providing real-time insights, Smart Skool fosters transparency, accountability, and improved collaboration within the school ecosystem.
